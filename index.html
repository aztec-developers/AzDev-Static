Why Aztec Developers?
Aztec Developers is a band of people looking to improve their overall knowledge in the constantly developing world of technology. We are here to learn new skills and have fun doing it, we welcome anyone looking to explore and innovate in this space. What we do is collaborate on teams in a hackathon format creating a fun environment where we compete and then vote on projects for the challenge at hand. The winning team gets to decide the next challenge that will take place keeping everyone involved and giving a chance to work on something they are passionate about.
